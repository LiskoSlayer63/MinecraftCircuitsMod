PulseLengthener(len):

Given a 1-tick pulse, this circuit outputs a "len"-tick pulse. In general, this circuit extends a "high" signal pulse by (len - 1) redstone ticks.

Example [PulseLengthener(2)]:

_~___~~____ Input
__~~__~~~__ Output

------------->
Redstone ticks

where '~' denotes a high signal
and '_' denotes a low signal
